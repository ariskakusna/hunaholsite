Is Glenn Maxwell married?

Yes, the Australian swashbuckling batsman tied the knot with his Indian-origin girlfriend, Vini Raman in March 2022.

The couple first married as per christian rituals on 18th March 2022 before tying the knot in a traditional Tamil-style wedding on March 27th.

Later, Maxwell's IPL franchise, the Royal Challengers Bangalore arranged a grand reception for the adorable couple during the 15th season of the lucrative T20 League.

On March 13, 2020, the two got engaged in a traditional Indian ceremony in Melbourne. Both dressed in Indian attire for the event with Maxwell donning a Sherwani.

Who is Vini Raman?

Vini Raman is a Melbourne-based pharmacist who was born and brought up in the city. She is one of the two daughters born to a South Indian, Hindu household. Raman is quite active on her social media platform and likes to give a sneak peek into her life, which majorly involves her beau, Glenn Maxwell. She rose to fame after Glenn Maxwell attended the Australian Cricket Awards in 2019 hand-in-hand with his lady love. Her favourite pastimes include swimming, travelling, and watching matches live from the stadium.

Where is Vini Raman from?

The future wife of Glenn Maxwell, Vini Raman hails from Melbourne, Australia. She was born and brought up there, where she completed her education in Medical Sciences and went on to become a pharmacist. She is very close to her family and often posts cute moments with her niece on her social media account.

How did Glenn Maxwell meet Vini Raman?

Though not much is known about their relationship history, it is believed that the duo met at a Melbourne Stars event and dated quite a while before finally getting engaged. In August 2017, Vini shared a picture on her Instagram account with Glenn Maxwell, which sparked some rumors of the two dating. Soon, the Australian cricketer started making regular appearances on her page, making the rumors more concrete.
