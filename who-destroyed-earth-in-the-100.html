Why did Alie destroy the world?

A.L.I.E. believes that the root problem of humanity is "too many people", seeing overpopulation as a threat to human survival. To solve this issue, in 2052, she launched a nuclear strike with the intention to save humanity from extinction by wiping out the majority of Earth's human inhabitants.

What caused the radiation in The 100?

It was caused by the failing nuclear power plants around the world which resulted in a wave of radiation spreading across the world. As a result of the rise in atmospheric radiation, the ground was uninhabitable for everyone except Nightbloods for the next five years.
