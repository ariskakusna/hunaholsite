How long can a trap arm be?

The maximum trap arm length is determined by the size of the pipe. For example, a 1-1/4 inch pipe may have a trap-arm length (the distance between the trap and the vent) of 30 inches. A trap-arm length of 10 feet may be achieved using a 4 inch pipe. As long as the pipe is at least 2 inches in diameter – with a maximum trap arm length of 5 feet – it should be suitable for 47 inches pipe.
