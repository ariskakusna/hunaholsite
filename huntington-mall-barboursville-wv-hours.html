What Time Does the Huntington Mall Open and Close?

The Huntington Mall opens at 10 a.m. and closes at 9 p.m. every day from Monday to Saturday. On Sunday, the mall opens at 11 a.m. and closes at 6 p.m. These hours apply to most stores in the mall, but it’s always best to contact the individual store to confirm their opening and closing hours.

Is the Huntington Mall Open on Saturday and Sunday?

Yes, the Huntington Mall is open on Saturdays and Sundays. As mentioned earlier, the mall opens at 10 a.m. on Saturdays and closes at 9 p.m. On Sundays, the mall opens at 11 a.m. and closes at 6 p.m. The mall operates on the same schedule every week, so you can always plan ahead.
