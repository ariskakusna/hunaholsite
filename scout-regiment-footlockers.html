Where To Find Scout Regiment Footlockers in Fortnite

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Scout Regiment Footlockers are the Attack on Titan event-themed chests, and here's a guide to finding them.

First Published April 13, 2023, 05:01

In order to unlock Eren Jaeger's skin in Fortnite, players have been asked to search the Scout Regiment footlocker. Fortnite 24.20 update has recently gone live with another round of Attack on Titan collaboration. The crossover brings the AOT's protagonist, Eren Jaeger, to Fortnite battle royale, and players must complete the battle pass quests in order to unlock his skin.

One of the tasks requires you to search the Scout Regiment footlocker. Although the challenge may sound pretty straightforward, you might not have heard about the term Scout Regiment footlocker before. Therefore, this essential guide covers all the steps to search the Scout Regiment footlocker and complete the quest quickly.

How To Find Scout Regiment Footlockers in Fortnite

Scout Regiment Footlockers are the Attack on Titan event-themed chests that can be found across multiple Fortnite locations, similar to the standard chests. So finding them is relatively easy on the island.

Nevertheless, if you can't find them, Jaeger’s Family Basement is the guaranteed location to come across Scout Regiment Footlockers. Jaeger's Family Basement can be found below the house at the southeast corner of Anvil Square.

Once you drop at the location and find this building, head inside it and locate the stairs leading down to the basement. There you'll find Jaeger's Family Basement for the quest.

It's worth noting that you'll need to open three Scout Regiment Footlockers in order to complete these quests. After that, the challenge will be marked as completed, and you'll receive Eren’s Faceoff Spray for your efforts.

So there you have it. That's everything you need to know about searching the Scout Regiment footlocker in Fortnite Chapter 4 Season 2.
