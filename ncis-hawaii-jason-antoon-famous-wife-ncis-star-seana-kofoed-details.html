    Doting parents to their two children – a son and a daughter – the pair often post snippets of their sweet family life on Instagram. The couple appears to live in Oahu, Hawai'i full-time, and in between filming, they love to spend quality time at the beach with their little ones, and their dog.

    Just an hour's drive from Vanessa Lachey's home, the couple have also spent plenty of time with their NCIS: Hawai'i co-stars outside of work. Back in November 2021, Jason revealed that they'd partied the night away at Vanessa and her husband Nick's epic Halloween bash along the North Shore. 

    The pair clearly enjoy working together on NCIS: Hawai'i, with Jason sharing the occasional behind-the-scenes photo of his wife. "Couples that work together dork together," he captioned the latest one.

    While little is known about the early days of their relationship, Jason has given fans a glimpse of their wedding, which took place in December 2010. Sharing a photo from their special day, the Ernie Malik actor could be seen wearing a classic navy suit. Meanwhile, the bride opted for a sleeveless halterneck gown with floral appliques and a cinched waist. 

    Swapping the traditional bridal veil for a single white flower that she'd placed in her loosely-curled hair – Seana looked absolutely stunning! According to Jason, they married in San Francisco City Hall on Christmas Eve.
