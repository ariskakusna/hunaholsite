Charlize Theron Wore a Plunging Black Bathing Suit

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Ignore the cheugy-adjacent (and mom-approved!) raffia visor, because Charlize Theron's sleek, sexy one-piece suit is the summer pool and beach inspiration that you need. While you may not have the opportunity to chill with your friends and family on a yacht à la Theron, you can try and slip into a bathing suit similar to hers, which features a deep V bodice alongside its stealthy all-black style.

According to Page Six, Theron anchored her yacht in Greece to celebrate her 46th birthday alongside her friends and family (she recently posted a rare video alongside her daughters Jackson and August, jumping into the clear blue Med). In addition to the visor, Theron wore a long gold pendant necklace and classic black sunglasses with her suit.

Over the weekend, Theron shared snapshots from her birthday celebration, which was prom-themed and came complete with a tutu and perm-filled murder-mystery add-on. She mentioned that she'd never had a prom growing up and wanted to rectify that with a celebration of her own.

"Well I never got to go to prom, but this year for my bday my friends decided to change that. I love these humans more than words can describe," she wrote on Instagram. "Only they could know that an 80s prom murder mystery party on a boat is my literal dream birthday. What a family, what a night."

Theron's pals Andy Cohen, January Jones, and Melanie Griffith wished her a very happy emoji-filled birthday in the comments.

Previously, she told InStyle that she has a love-hate relationship with aging, noting that she has good days and bad days when she sees herself — because celebrities really are just like us sometimes (minus the yachts).

"There are days when I really love my face and have no issues with the wrinkles around my mouth or eyes," she shared. "Then there are days we tell ourselves, 'Oh my god, maybe it's time to get a face-lift.' And that's OK."
