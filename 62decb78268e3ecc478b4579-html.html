Ben Affleck was seen teary-eyed while dining during Jennifer Lopez's 53 birthday celebration

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopez went to Paris to celebrate Jlo's 53 birthday.

Media outlet Page Six revealed pictures of an emotional Ben Affleck while dining with her new wife.

The images illustrate a teary-eyed Affleck moved by the conversation shared with Jennifer Lopez.

Affleck and Lopez attended La Girafe, a gourmet restaurant located in front of the Eiffel Tower.

According to Page Six, Jennifer Lopez consoled her new spouse while the actor was getting emotional.

JLo "grabbed Affleck's face close to her chest" to try to console him, Page Six mentioned.

Affleck wore a black suit with a button-down shirt opened at the neckline.

Meanwhile, Jennifer Lopez wore a "gown with cutouts that she accessorized with a large pearl necklace," as page six described.
