FAQs

How can I get Rhoback free shipping code?

You can get free standard shipping on orders of $150 or more. Once shipped, your order should be with you within 8-12 working days. If your order is less than $150, there is a $5 standard shipping charge.  

When are the Rhoback sales?

Start shopping polos, hoodies, and shirts for less with the Rhoback seasonal sales. Whilst there is no sale menu, you can shop the sale by typing ‘Sale’ into the search bar. We’ve previously seen discounts of up to 80% off hoodies. Check the sales during Black Friday as well for some of the best discounts at Rhoback. 

Does Rhoback have a military discount?

Yes. Sign up for the military discount at Rhoback for 15% off your online order. This discount is for both military staff, veterans, and first responders. Confirm your military ID at the checkout via ID.me.  

Does Rhoback have a first responder discount?

Rhoback does have 15% off for first responders. This can be used on all your online orders, from Barstool polos to hoodies. You will need to confirm your work status via ID.me on the checkout page. Rhoback will then send your first responder discount to you. 

Does Rhoback offer free returns?

Returns can be made for free to Rhoback within 30 days of purchase. Your products should be unwashed, unworn, and with their original tags attached. It’s worth noting natural wear and tear or accidental damage will not be covered by the Rhoback Quality Promise.

Refer Your Friends: You can also help your friends or family save on their next golf shirt, polo, or hoodie with a referral code. You will need to enter your name and preferred email address to get your exclusive code. When your friend orders with your code, they will get $20 off their first order of $50 or more. You will also get $20 off your next order. Share your referral link by text, email, and even social media to gain the most savings. 

Sign Up for Emails: You can get 10% off your first order when you sign up for emails. You’ll also be sent sale updates, restock notifications, and be kept updated on new polos and apparel. Simply click on the relevant promo code on this page to access this discount. You can also go to the Rhoback website and click ‘10% off Your First Order’ from the footer. This can help you save on your new sports apparel and essentials. Rhoback might occasionally send you an exclusive coupon too, so you can save on future orders. Be sure to check this page for the latest promotions and sign-up discounts.

Shop the Black Friday and Cyber Monday sales: Rhoback runs a Black Friday and Cyber Monday sale every year, and this year is no exception. Head to the Rhoback website to take an extra 20% off your online order this November.  

How to use your Rhoback discount code

  • Select your Rhoback discount code from this page. 
  • Next, click ‘Get Coupon’ or ‘Get Deal’ to see your discount code. 
  • Click ‘Get Offer at Rhoback’ to continue to the Rhoback website. 
  • Add your sports apparel or casual clothing to your cart.  
  • Click ‘Checkout’ on the menu that should appear. 
  • You should find the ‘Gift Card or discount code’ box above your order total. 
  • Copy and paste your Rhoback discount code into this box. 
  • Click ‘Apply’ and enjoy your sports clothing for less.
