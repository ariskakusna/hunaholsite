Who is Jodie Foster's wife, Alexandra Hedison?

Alexandra Hedison is an American fine arts photographer.

She was born in Los Angeles in 1969 to parents David Hedison and Bridget Mori.

She went on to attend the State University of New York at Purchase and the University of California.

Hedison's work has been shown in solo and group exhibitions in galleries and museums across the US and Europe.
