Shake That Ass

August 2024 · 2 minute read

After hitting us with “Hammertime” earlier this month with ManMan Savage, rising ATL rapper Key! decides to return to the scene today and share another record called “Shake That Ass,” which is produced by Franchise Did It.

It’s unclear at the moment on where this record will end up, but hopefully there’s more on the way, or better yet a project for that matter. Hit play and let us know what you think.

In other related news, if you happen to be in the Florida area next week be sure to check out Key! on his “Florida Run” next week. (cop tix here)
