Alex Cooper’s Early Life

Alex Cooper was born on August 21, 1994, in Newtown, Pennsylvania. She attended Boston University, where she played soccer. After graduating, she moved to New York City and started working as an on-air host for Dirty Water Media.

Start of “Call Her Daddy”

In 2018, Cooper and her then-roommate Sofia Franklyn started the podcast “Call Her Daddy.” The podcast, which discusses relationships, sex, and social scenes, quickly gained popularity due to its raw and unfiltered content.

Deal with Barstool Sports

Shortly after the launch of “Call Her Daddy,” Barstool Sports, a digital media company, offered Cooper and Franklyn a deal. This partnership significantly boosted the podcast’s reach and popularity, leading to increased revenue.

Controversy and Solo Hosting

In 2020, a contract dispute between Cooper, Franklyn, and Barstool Sports led to a temporary halt of the podcast. Eventually, Cooper signed a new contract with Barstool Sports to continue the podcast as a solo host.

Revenue from Podcasting

As the host of “Call Her Daddy,” Cooper earns a significant income. Her deal with Barstool Sports reportedly includes a base salary, bonuses based on podcast downloads, and merchandise sales.

Spotify Deal

In 2021, Cooper signed a multi-year deal with Spotify worth $60 million. This deal is expected to significantly increase her net worth in the coming years.

Income from Social Media

Cooper is also a social media influencer with a large following on Instagram. She earns additional income through sponsored posts and brand partnerships.

Merchandise Sales

“Call Her Daddy” merchandise, which includes clothing and accessories, is another source of income for Cooper. The merchandise is popular among the podcast’s listeners, contributing to Cooper’s wealth.

Real Estate Investments
